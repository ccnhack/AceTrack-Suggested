The Arsenal

Powerful tools designed for the modern sports ecosystem.

🎾 Matchmaking

Intelligent Skill-Based Matchmaking

Our proprietary algorithms analyze your performance, consistency, and playstyle to instantly connect you with players at your exact skill level.

  • TrueSkill™ rating system with glicko-2 confidence intervals
  • Challenge friends or accept open global invitations
  • Location-aware court booking integration
🏆 Tournaments

End-to-End Tournament Management

From weekend shootouts to national championships — generate dynamic brackets, process registrations, and broadcast live scoring to the cloud.

  • Automated bracket generation with seeding logic
  • Push notifications for match schedules & court assignments
  • Real-time live scoring visible to all participants
📹 AI Analytics

AI-Powered Video Analytics

Stop guessing — start improving. Upload match recordings and let our AI break down your mechanics frame by frame, delivering actionable insights.

  • Automated footwork, racket speed & shot placement analysis
  • AI-generated highlight reels for scouts & coaches
  • Historical trend tracking with progression graphs
