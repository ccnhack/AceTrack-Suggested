Frequently Asked Questions

Is AceTrack free for players?
Yes! Players can register, track stats, and join public matchmaking for free. Premium features like advanced AI video analytics require a subscription.
How does the matchmaking rating work?
We use a proprietary algorithm that calculates your rating based on win/loss records, opponent skill level, and historical consistency to ensure balanced matches.
Are my match recordings private?
Absolutely. All video uploads are strictly private by default. You have full control over whether you want to share them with a coach, an academy, or keep them for personal review.
